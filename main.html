Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Social Media Automation-IG ("the App"), operated by TiwanaTech, collects, uses, and protects information when providing automated Instagram comment management services to connected business accounts.

1. Information We Collect

When an Instagram business account connects to the App, we collect and process the following data through the Instagram API:

  • Public comments posted on the connected account's media (comment text, comment ID, commenter username)
  • Media identifiers of posts belonging to the connected account
  • The connected account's Instagram account ID and username

We do not collect passwords, private messages beyond what is required for the service, payment information, or data from accounts that are not connected to the App.

2. How We Use Information

Collected data is used solely to:

  • Generate and post automated replies to comments on the connected account's posts
  • Prevent duplicate replies to the same comment
  • Maintain an audit log of processed comments for the connected business

Comment text may be processed by third-party AI services (OpenAI) for the sole purpose of generating a reply. Data is not used for advertising, profiling, or sold to any third party.

3. Data Storage and Security

Processed comment records are stored in a secured cloud database (MongoDB Atlas) with access restricted to authorized infrastructure. Access tokens are stored securely and never exposed publicly. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Comment records are retained for as long as the business account remains connected to the App, for audit and duplicate-prevention purposes. A connected business may request deletion of all its stored data at any time by contacting us at the address below. Data deletion requests are honored within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Services

The App relies on the following processors:

  • Meta Platforms (Instagram API) — receiving comment events and posting replies
  • OpenAI — generating reply text from comment content
  • MongoDB Atlas — data storage

Each processor handles data under its own privacy policy and security practices.

6. User Rights

Connected account owners may disconnect the App at any time via Instagram's "Apps and Websites" settings, which revokes the App's access. You may also request a copy or deletion of stored data by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest revision.
